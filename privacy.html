Privacy Policy

DeepTree respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we protect it.

Our Commitment

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. We collect the minimum information necessary and never sell, share, or trade your personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymous analytics to understand how our website is used. We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly service that:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track you across websites
  • Is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR

The only data collected is aggregate information such as page views, referrer sources, and general geographic region (country level). This data cannot be used to identify you.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information (unless you email us directly)
  • IP addresses
  • Device fingerprints
  • Any information that could identify you personally

Third Parties

We do not share any information with third parties. We do not use advertising networks, social media trackers, or any other third-party services that collect personal data.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Aggregate analytics data is kept for historical analysis only.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. If you have contacted us via email, you may request deletion of that correspondence at any time.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at team@deeptree.com.
